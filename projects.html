Pitching Machine Umpire

CONTEXT: There are a number of small-ball pitching machines on the market which are used to help hitting coaches. The machines pitch plastic-ractice golf-balls 40+ miles per hour. They are tremendous for building hand-eye coordination (and they are a blast). And so a player can get better at putting the bat on the ball. QUESTION: But how does a player evaluate whether the pitch they took was a ball or strike? Or the one on which they swung, was a ball or strike but for their swing?

Objective: Build an app that, with 3 inexpensive cameras, can be trained to call balls and strikes as follows:
  1. Whether a pitch TAKEN is a ball or a strike.
  2. Whether or Not a SWINGING STRIKE ave been a called strike if otherwise.
